Privacy Policy for Multi-View Category Chart

Last updated: April 30, 2026

About This Visual

Multi-View Category Chart is a Microsoft Power BI custom visual that renders a small-multiples grid of pie, donut, or bar charts driven by a category and series. It is published to Microsoft AppSource and is developed and maintained by Ahmad Ibrahim.

Data Collection

Multi-View Category Chart does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or user data.

  • The visual processes data entirely locally within the user's Power BI session (Power BI Desktop, Power BI Service, or embedded environment).
  • No data bound to the visual is ever sent to the developer, to any external server, or to any third-party service.
  • The visual does not use cookies, local storage, session storage, or any browser-based tracking mechanism for analytics or advertising.
  • The visual makes no outbound network requests (no fetch, XMLHttpRequest, WebSocket, or beacon calls).
  • The visual does not include any analytics, telemetry, or tracking SDKs.

How Your Data Is Used

All data fields you bind to Multi-View Category Chart (categories, series, and measures) are read from the Power BI data model in memory and used solely to render the visual on screen. The data never leaves the Power BI host environment.

Third-Party Services

Multi-View Category Chart does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics providers. The only runtime dependencies are open-source rendering libraries (D3.js, React) that execute locally in the user's browser as part of the visual itself.

Microsoft Power BI

As Multi-View Category Chart runs inside Microsoft Power BI, the broader handling of your Power BI data is governed by Microsoft's privacy policies. Please refer to the Microsoft Privacy Statement for details on how Microsoft handles data within Power BI.

Children's Privacy

Multi-View Category Chart is a business intelligence tool not directed at children under the age of 13. No personal information is knowingly collected from any user, including children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of Multi-View Category Chart after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
